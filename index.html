About Sam

Sam Altman is a visionary entrepreneur, investor, and technology leader who has played a pivotal role in shaping the future of artificial intelligence and startup ecosystems globally.

As the CEO of OpenAI, Sam leads one of the world's premier AI research laboratories focused on ensuring that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity. Prior to OpenAI, Sam served as the president of Y Combinator, where he helped launch and mentor thousands of startups that have collectively shaped the modern technology landscape.

Throughout his career, Sam has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to fostering innovation, supporting entrepreneurs, and addressing the most pressing technological challenges of our time. His unique perspective on AI safety, technological progress, and the future of humanity has made him one of the most influential voices in technology today.
